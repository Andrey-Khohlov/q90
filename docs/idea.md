An online service is being developed: 

**database of coffee roasters, farmers, user ratings, and coffee discussion chat.**

# Technology Stack
- Backend: FastAPI (Python)
- Frontend: Flet (Python-based UI framework)
- Database: PostgreSQL with SQLAlchemy ORM
- Containerization: Docker & Docker Compose
- Migration: Alembic
- Asynchronous Processing: asyncio

# Goals:
- Obtaining expert independent peer review from the community for roasted coffee
- community evaluation of coffee
- communication: discussions under the post with a description of the coffee rating, general chat
- getting recommendations 
- tracking the taste experience of friends
- a brewing diary to track individual experiences and the effect of brewing methods on taste
- traceability of coffee
- comparison of coffee quality and price
- communication between farmers, roasters and coffee lovers 

# Services:
- Coffee database with detailed information (origin, processing, tasting notes, etc.)
- User accounts and profiles
- registration via social networks OAuth 2.0
- Rating (scales: 0-1, 1-3, 1-5, 1-10, 1-100) and review system
- Discussion forums/chats about specific coffees
- Search and filtering capabilities
- AI filling out the coffee card by following the link
- message moderation (bot, complain button)
- localization (front, coffee bases, messages)
